Fix Actions
Mar 2 · 12:26 Trigger: An unusual data combination activated a dormant infinite loop bug. The process crashed. Because checkpoint saving was broken, every restart replayed from this exact moment — a self-reinforcing crash loop, 185 times total.
Mar 4 · 16:00 Merchant balance process crashes: Same root cause in a separate process. On restart it replayed 51.5 hours of historical deposits, inflating balance displays until the replay finished naturally at 20:02. Actual funds were never affected.
